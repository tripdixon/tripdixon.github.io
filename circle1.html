Solution

1. To view the console, press Command + Option + J (Mac) or Control + Shift + J (Windows, Linux, Chrome OS).
2. Click the "Let's Do It!" button to begin logging to the console every 10 seconds.
3. Click the "Let's Stop It!" button to stop the logging.
